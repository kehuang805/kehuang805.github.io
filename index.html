About Me

My name is Kevin Huang and I am a Chinese American born and raised in San Francisco. I originally studied International Business at San Francisco State University where I received my Bachelors from in 2019, graduating as the department honoree. My interest in starting a career in a computer science related field began when I took a few Java courses at my local community college to make use of my time after graduation. The advent of COVID and its severe effects on our society gave me a push to take the leap in pursuing a career in the industry and I decided to enroll in Rithm School's web development program.

Interests

Outside of programming, some of my hobbies include reading, playing games, watching anime, and learning Japanese. I have been learning Japanese since high school and continued throughout college, receiving my minor degree. In 2017, I passed the highest level (N1) of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test, scoring in the 98th percentile globally so I have certified fluency! Like most things however, there is always more to learn and I am always excited to see something I don't know in Japanese, whether it be a word, an idiom, or something about the culture.

Although it is quite different from studying traditional languages, I look forward to the abundance of opportunities I will have in the future to learn more about programming languages!
